ABOUT US

Unique, life-changing experiences

We believe you deserve a unique experience off the beaten track, with country highlights, hand-picked comfortable accommodation, and expert local guides. We focus on historical and cultural Asian destinations, exploring interesting ways to travel for families, couples, and annual vacations. Every hotel is carefully selected out of over 100 similar hotels. Each local guide serving you is well-trained and will help you immerse into a world you are waiting to discover.

Our Values

Asia Highlights' vision is creating beautiful journeys, for our discerning customers as well as for us.It was in 1998 that Victor Shu started China Highlights as a way to help Westerner individuals discover the real China. As a tour guide for 4 years, he had been fed up with guiding foreign travelers in large groups, merely scratching the surface of China on inflexible tourist trap tours. He initiated a new way of travel in China: private tours, created to match your own interests and budget
